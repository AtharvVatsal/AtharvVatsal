# 💫 About Me
🔭 **Passionate about Machine Learning, Computer Vision, and Web Development.** 🎓 Computer Science student at **Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT)**, combining core systems knowledge with cutting-edge AI research.

🤖 **Featured Projects & Research:**
- **DriveSense:** Building autonomous driving perception systems combining YOLOv8 object detection and U-Net semantic segmentation.
- **RiskGrid:** Developing ML models for spatio-temporal crime prediction.
- **IRBn/Bn Police Report Processor:** Implementing advanced NLP (DistilBERT) to process, analyze, and summarize official police reports.
- **Physics & AI Research:** Conducting research on Neural Networks & Dark Matter Phenomenology, with recognition from IEEE and institutional collaborations.
- **Full-Stack Development:** Currently designing and deploying a comprehensive portfolio website to showcase my software engineering and visual arts projects.

📸 **Event & Concert Photographer** Covered notable artists like Sonu Sood, Shreya Ghoshal, Mohan Sisters, and Kinjal Chatterjee. *Digital gallery coming soon to my portfolio!*
